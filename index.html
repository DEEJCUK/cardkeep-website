Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

CardKeep is designed with your privacy as our top priority. Here's how we handle your precious memories:

Local Storage

All your card data, photos, and personal information are stored locally on your iPhone using Core Data. We never send your data to our servers or any third parties.

iCloud Photo Sync

When you save cards to CardKeep, the photos are automatically added to your device's photo library in an album called "CardKeep". If you have iCloud Photos enabled in your device settings, these photos will sync to your iCloud account as part of your normal photo backup - this is handled entirely by Apple, not by us.

No Data Collection

We don't collect analytics, crash reports, or any personal information. Your memories are yours alone.

Permissions

CardKeep only requests camera and photo library permissions to save your card photos. These are essential for the app's functionality.
