üì± Data & Backup Questions

What happens if I delete the app?
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: If you delete CardKeepApp, all your saved card data will be lost forever! This includes all the information you entered (sender names, occasions, dates, tags, notes).
However, your photos are safe! All the card photos you took are automatically saved to your iPhone's Photos app in an album called "CardKeepApp". These photos will remain on your device even if you delete the app.
Are my cards backed up anywhere?
Your card data (text information) is only stored locally on your device - we don't have any servers or cloud backup. However:

Photos: If you have iCloud Photos enabled, your card photos automatically sync to iCloud as part of your regular photo backup.

App data: If you have iCloud Backup enabled for your device, the app data may be included in your device backup, but this isn't guaranteed to restore perfectly.
Can I transfer my cards to a new phone?
Photos: Yes! If you use iCloud Photos, your card photos will automatically appear on your new device.

Card data: This is tricky. Your card information might transfer if you restore from an iCloud or iTunes backup, but it's not guaranteed. Unfortunately, there's no manual export/import feature currently.

üí° Pro tip: Before switching phones, make sure you have iCloud Photos enabled so at least your card photos transfer safely!
How much storage does the app use?
The app itself is small, but storage depends on how many cards you save and photo quality:

‚Ä¢ App size: About 10-20MB
‚Ä¢ Per card: 2-10MB depending on photo quality
‚Ä¢ 100 cards: Roughly 200MB-1GB

Photos are also saved to your Photos app, so they count twice against your storage (once in CardKeepApp, once in Photos).

Why can't I rotate the app to landscape mode?
CardKeepApp is designed exclusively for portrait mode. The interface is optimized for iPhone's portrait orientation to provide the best experience for viewing and managing greeting cards.

The card layouts, photo viewing, and overall design work best when your phone is held normally (vertically).

What if I want a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store. You can:

1. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases
2. Tap View Account
3. Find your purchase and request a refund

Or visit Apple's Report a Problem website.

Apple typically offers refunds within a reasonable timeframe if you're not satisfied.
Do you offer family sharing?
Yes! CardKeepApp supports Apple's Family Sharing. One purchase can be shared with up to 6 family members.

However, remember that each person's cards are stored locally on their own device - family members won't see each other's card collections.
